# Sales-DataAnalysis-and-Data-Prediciton
Analysing Sales Data and Predicting the Sales for the Future Fiscal Quarters <br> <br>
Sales data analysis done on the CSV file provided in the repository. <br> <br>
The problems addressed in the Jupyter Notebook are:- <br>
1. What are the overall sales trend? <br>
2. What are the top 10 most selling business units and product families? <br>
3. Which is the most preferred Business Unit or and Product Family? <br>
4. Fiscal Year wise sales <br>
5. Predicting the next few Fiscal Quarters's Booked_Qty <br>
